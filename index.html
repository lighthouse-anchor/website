Our Mission

Lighthouse Anchor was created to provide information and resources to children affected by abuse. We are here to help you and show you there is a way out, even in dark times.

This website is a hub for all of our resources. With this website, you can access Lighthouse Anchor's pamphlets, links, and other materials that aren't available on paper. We aim to help you identify various kinds of abuse, ultimately allowing you to properly understand your situation and how you can get help. Our materials include descriptions of unknown terms, answers to what if questions, and further resources you can use to get more help. We are here to guide you, and although we cannot predict what the outcome will be when you speak up, we can help you understand the realities and benefits of taking action against abuse.
